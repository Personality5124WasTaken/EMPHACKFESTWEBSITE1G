Addressing Climate Change

The Challenge Ahead

Climate change brings a large threat to our planet. Rising temperatures, big weather events, and ocean level rise are just a few of the consequences. It's important that we take action to lower these effects and work towards a habitable future.

Our Product

Everyone can help with the fight against climate change. actions like lowering energy use, recycling, and supporting sustainable practices can make a change.but these actions aren't as simple especially if you do them for a long time. Thats why we've made a product to help and try stopping climate change. A trash can! No this is not a joke. This trash can is able to help delay climate change as much as possible, because the people who are using this item will be able to compost items in the correct orderly fashion. This helps decrease climate change because around 55% of waste goes into landfills, which has tons of methane in them. These wastes trap tons of heat in the atmosphere making the climate warmer.

Stay Informed

Stay informed about climate issues and solutions. Use our product to lower greenhouse gases being released in landfills, Follow reputable sources, attend local events, and engage in discussions to raise awareness. Together, we can make a positive impact on our environment.
